In this kind of tape, heavy selling does not always end a move, but it does make breakouts less durable if the chart starts to wobble.

The next catalyst cluster arrives quickly. JKS reports on April 16, SLS has AACR exposure on April 21, QS follows on April 22, and late April gets crowded with ENVX and STEM on April 29 and BBAI, BE, and FIVN on April 30. Bottom line: this board is tradable, but it is still a proof market; the setups with real dates and real cash can work, while fragile narratives with weak tape still need to be sized as risk trades, not convictions.
